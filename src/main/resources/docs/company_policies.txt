Section: Annual Leave Policy

Every employee is entitled to 18 paid leaves per calendar year.

Unused leaves cannot be carried forward to the next year.

Leave approval is subject to project requirements and manager approval.


Section: Work From Home (WFH) Policy

Employees are allowed to work from home up to 3 days per week.

Work From Home is not permitted during the probation period.

WFH requests must be pre-approved by the reporting manager.


Section: Laptop Allocation Policy

The company provides official laptops based on role:

Developers are provided with MacBook Pro 14-inch systems.

QA Team Members are provided with Dell Latitude laptops.

All devices are company property and must be returned upon exit.


Section: Health Insurance Policy

Each employee is covered under a Health Insurance Policy of ₹8 lakh per year.

Insurance coverage is applicable only to the employee.

Parents are not included in the insurance coverage.
